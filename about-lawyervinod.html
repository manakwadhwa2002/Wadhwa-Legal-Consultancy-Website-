Advocate Vinod Wadhwa

Advocate Vinod Wadhwa is the founder of Wadhwa Legal Consultancy and has distinguished himself as one of the leading personal injury lawyers in New Delhi focusing his practice on preparing and trying personal injury cases including car accidents, truck accidents, pedestrian accidents, and slip and fall matters. Over the years, Vinod has obtained lakhs of rupees in settlements and verdicts for his clients and has published articles on personal injury law. Vinod values that his work allows him to make a direct, positive impact on the lives of people who have experienced stressful or traumatic events, such as a serious injury in an accident. He has a genuine compassion for helping clients who need the services Wadhwa Legal Consultancy offers.

Over the course of a decade, Vinod has applied his entrepreneurial spirit to growing the firm into a talented team of experienced New Delhi lawyers and paralegals in order to better serve the diverse legal needs of people in Delhi City and the surrounding region.

Vinod Wadhwa received a Bachelor of Law and Bachelor of Commerce degree from Maharishi Dayanand University
