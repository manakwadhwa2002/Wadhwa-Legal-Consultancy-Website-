Prof. O.P. Wadhwa

    Prof. Om Parkash Wadhwa did his M.A. (Public Administration), M.A. (Political Science), M.Phil (Public Administration), scoring high marks in the Dissertation titled ‘University Administration in Haryana’ from University of Rajasthan, Jaipur; Diploma in Administrative Law, Diploma in Labour Law (obtained under the benign signatures of the then Chief Justice of India — Mr. Justice Y.V Chandrachud), conducted research during the Course of Administrative Law on the topic of “Natural Justice in Unfairmeans Cases in the University Examinations” under the supervision of the then Director (equivalent to Vice-Chancellor), Dr. (Mrs) Professor Alice Jacob (who later became Member, Law Commission, Government of India), Qualified NET (Public Administration), PhD thesis titled “UNIVERSITY ADMINISTRATION IN INDIA” submitted to Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra,

  • Service

    • Prof. Wadhwa served in Government Colleges of Haryana for about 28 years as Head, Department of Public Administration, and served as Law Officer of the College for about 22 years.

  • Achievements

    • During the Service period, Professor Wadhwa has handled Civil suits in lower courts, Writ Petitions in the P&H High Court, Chandigarh and also several Special Leave Appeals (SLPs) in the Supreme Court of India. Further during this service period, about 250 papers (Articles/editor’s mail of Professor Wadhwa have been published, mainly on ‘University Administration’ in journals, in the Tribune, Chandigarh, NCR Tribune, New Delhi and ‘University Today’, New Delhi, etc.; has also attended about 200 Seminars, Summits, Conferences (International, National & Regional) in different parts of the country {e.g. Shimla, Chandigarh, Patiala, Delhi, Jaipur, Jodhpur, Ajmer, Gwalior, Lucknow, Nanital, and Chennai (Tamilnadu) etc.}, Qualified RTI Online Certificate Course (OCC) organised by the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances, Govt. of India in collaboration with Centre for Good Governance, Hyderbad and has been placed at 6th position in India in the year 2012; organised Seminars/Extension Lecture; delivered Extension Lectures during and after retirement, organised Legal Literacy Functions—Intra College, District Level, Division Level and one at State Level and during the State Level Legal Literacy Function begged trophy & obtained the same under the benevolent hands of Chief Minister of Haryana, Supreme Court & High Court Judges, defended College legal cases in District Court, High Court and Supreme Court as has been mentioned in the briefing of Wadhwa Legal Consultancy (WLC)

  • RTI Applications Filed

    • Prof. Wadhwa has filed about 300 RTI Applications out of which bulk of the RTI Applications have been filed before the State Public Information Officers of Ch. Devi Lal University (CDLU), Sirsa and Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak and has been filing RTI requests in different parts of the country; has been filing First Appeals & Second Appeals before the First Appellate Authorities (FAAs) and Central Information Commission, State Information Commissions (SICs); has been giving consultancy on RTI and service matters, physically & online for the last 14-15 years .
